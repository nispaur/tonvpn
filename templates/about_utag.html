{% blocktrans with PNAME as pname %}

UTag is a short keyword meaning UniqueTag. This tag allows a simple way of communicating between {{ pname }} users and administrators when working with orders and payments.

Indeed, an UTag is generated for each payments we request, so that you can write this tag on your bank checks or bank transfert comments. Administrators will be able to recognize whose payment it is thanks to this tag.

So, every time you need to make a non-automated payment, please ensure you join the corresponding UTag so {{ pname }} staff can validate your request.
